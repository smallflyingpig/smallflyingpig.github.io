Jiguo Li

I received the B.E. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2016. I earned the Ph.D. in Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Science in 2022, under the supervision by Prof. Wen Gao and Prof. Siwei Ma . From 2022 to 2023, I am a post-doctor researcher in Micro nano system center, Fudan university, with the co-supervisor Prof. Lirong Zheng. Now I am a CV/NLP engineer in JD.inc. My research interests focus on AIGC, including LLMs, image generation and multimodal generation.
