Jiguo Li

I received the B.E. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2016. I earned the Ph.D. in Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Science in 2022, under the supervision by Prof. Wen Gao and Prof. Siwei Ma. From 2022 to 2023, I was a post-doctor researcher in Fudan University. Now I am a senior engineer in MeiTuan on LLM and its applications.
